Meet the Main Characters of Divine Academy

By MC Perry ✦ March 25, 2026

Welcome to the first official deep dive inside the Grimoire! Since the world of Divine Academy is packed with volatile magic, shifting alliances, and extremely morally grey individuals, I thought it was time we formally introduced the lineup.

If you love a dark academy setting where the FMC relies on her wits rather than brute strength, and her brooding professors and classmates have a devastating hidden agenda, you are going to fall completely in love with this chaotic group. Without further ado, let's meet the core roster.

Katrina Amerie

✦ ☽ ◯ ☾ ✦

Katrina Amerie survived being abandoned—now the gods want to decide whether she’s worth claiming or destroying.

Katrina was abandoned at birth and raised in foster care with only a children’s mythology book and a sealed letter she was forbidden to open until her eighteenth birthday. Growing up without answers taught her how to endure, not hope. She became independent out of necessity, learned to hide fear behind sarcasm, and trained herself never to rely on anyone but herself.

Miles

✦ ☽ ◯ ☾ ✦

Miles was raised to conquer—but losing her is the one battle he refuses to fight.

Miles is the heir to the Earth Kingdom, bred for war and raised to rule. Broad-shouldered and imposing, with tattoos etched across his skin like declarations of ownership and power, he carries himself with the confidence of someone who has never been allowed to doubt his strength. As the son of Ares and Athena, Miles was taught that dominance keeps order and that weakness invites destruction.

Beckett

Aphelion

✦ ☽ ◯ ☾ ✦

Aphelion was born to rule the skies—but falling for her is the first choice he’s ever made for himself.

Aphelion, heir to the Sky Kingdom, wears charm as easily as lightning dances at his fingertips. With fiery red hair, an easy smile, and a confidence that draws people in, he has always been a natural leader, raised to command attention and loyalty without trying. As the son of Zeus and Hera, expectations have followed him since birth, shaping every decision before he was old enough to make one for himself.

Samael

✦ ☽ ◯ ☾ ✦

Samael has seen every ending worth fearing—loving her is the one future he can’t predict.

Samael is the heir to the Underworld, raised in shadows and shaped by silence. With long black hair often tied back, pale skin marked by ritual tattoos, and raven-black wings that set him apart even among gods, he carries himself with blunt honesty and little patience for pretense. As the son of Hades and Persephone, Samael inherited blue Underworld fire and the burden of premonition, cursed to see only negative outcomes and never the moments worth hoping for.

Luka

✦ ☽ ◯ ☾ ✦

Luka teaches control because chaos is the one thing he’s afraid to unleash.

Professor Luka is tall and sharp-featured. With an easy confidence and a presence that commands attention, he moves through the world as someone entirely comfortable with who he is. Descended from Loki and Eris, Luka was raised with an understanding of fluidity—of power, identity, and desire—long before he ever learned the truth of his lineage.

Who are you claiming?

I cannot wait to hear who your favorite is. Drop your early predictions in my DMs or on TikTok, because trust me—none of them are exactly who they seem in Book 1.
