Meet the Main Characters of Divine Academy

By MC Perry ✦ March 25, 2026

Welcome to the first official deep dive inside the Grimoire! Since the world of Divine Academy is packed with volatile magic, shifting alliances, and extremely morally grey individuals, I thought it was time we formally introduced the lineup.

If you love a dark academy setting where the FMC relies on her wits rather than brute strength, and her brooding professors and classmates have a devastating hidden agenda, you are going to fall completely in love with this chaotic group. Without further ado, let's meet the core roster.

Katrina Amerie

"Katrina Amerie survived being abandoned—now the gods want to decide whether she’s worth claiming or destroying."

Katrina was abandoned at birth and raised in foster care with only a children’s mythology book and a sealed letter she was forbidden to open until her eighteenth birthday. Growing up without answers taught her how to endure, not hope. She became independent out of necessity, learned to hide fear behind sarcasm, and trained herself never to rely on anyone but herself.
